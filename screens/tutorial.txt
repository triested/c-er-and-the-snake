Move through the dungeon using the WASD keys.

Fire your weapon using the arrow keys.

Dungeon key:
         .   Floor    
         #   Walls    
         G   Gold     
         H   Health   
         x   Enemies  
         S   stairs   
         @   you!     

Enemies will hurt you if they move on you,
                           don't let them!

Gold is valuable because of how good it is
    at conducting electricity! Collect it!
    
Make your way to the stairs in order to   
               escape from the dungeon!
